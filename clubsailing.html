Club Sailing

Club Sailing is the non-competitive side of our club.  We host lake days, trips, and other fun events for the general membership.

Our Fleet

Georgia Tech owns a fleet of sailboats, including dinghies, keelboats, and windsurfers, up at Lake Lanier Sailing Club.  Our fleet is available for use by any member who is skipper certified.
