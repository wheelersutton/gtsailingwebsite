Welcome!

We are excited to announce our 2nd Annual Georgia Tech Open Regatta. We had a very successful event last year and are looking forward to another fun event this year. This event is open to all ages and we welcome anybody to come and watch the races. Racing will be followed by dinner and awards. Keep scrolling for more information regarding event logistics, registration, gear orders, etc. We are excited to bring this event back and hope to see you on April 14th!

If you are interested in sponsorship opportunities or have questions, please reach out to gatechopenregatta@gmail.com.

Regatta Information

We are looking forward to holding races in 3 fleets this year: C420, Laser, and Opti. Competition is open to all ages. All participants in the regatta will receive an event t-shirt, dinner, and a bagged lunch.

Fleets

Laser

Racing will be in a traditional fleet race format. Competitors are expected to provide their own boat. Boat charters will be available on a limited basis.

C420

SIGNATURE EVENT

Racing will be collegiate-style with rotations every two races. Experience what it's like to sail a collegiate racing format. Open to individuals or teams of two.

Opti

Racing will be in a traditional fleet race format. Competitors are expected to provide their own boat. Juniors only.
