Welcome!

Join us at Lake Lanier Sailing Club on March 30th for our 3rd Annual Georgia Tech Open Regatta! This is a fun, family-oriented event open to all ages. We will run the C420 and Opti Fleets as usual. Teams of two are required for the C420 fleet and juniors only in the Opti fleet. New this year is the J/24 and Melges 24 combined fleet. The only catch is you must have 1 Georgia Tech Sailor or GT Alumnus as a crew member on your boat. Need help finding crew? We can help with that! We look forward to seeing everyone on March 30th at Lake Lanier!

Regatta Information

We will be holding races in 3 fleets this year: C420, Opti, and J/24 + Melges 24. Competition is open to all ages.

Fleets

C420

Racing will be collegiate-style with rotations every two races. Experience what it's like to sail a collegiate racing format. Open to teams of two. The regatta chairs can assist in matching single skippers and crew.

Optimist

Racing will be in a traditional fleet race format. Competitors are expected to provide their own boat. Juniors only.

J/24 + Melges 24

NEW THIS YEAR! Racing will be conducted on the same course with separate scoring for each class. Competitors are expected to provide their own boat. Someone affiliated with Georgia Tech must be part of your crew. J/24 chartering is available on a limited basis.
