How to Join

All undergraduate students, graduate students, and faculty at Georgia Tech can join the Sailing Club.  No prior sailing experience is necessary.  We accept new members year-round.
  1. CONTACT US - get on our email list so you'll receive important club updates.  You can also join our Facebook group for updates.
  2. WEEKLY MEETINGS - join us for a weekly meeting in the CRC. We usually meet Mondays at 7:30pm, but make sure to check our Facebook page first. During meetings, we discuss upcoming sailing opportunities and events.
  3. PAY DUES - Sailing Club dues are $65 per semester for undergraduate and graduate students and $95 for faculty (dues are lowered to $55 and $75 respectively during summer semester).  Dues can be paid in cash check, or card to the Treasurer during a meeting, through Venmo (@gtsailing), or through PayPal.
