FUNDRAISING

The Sailing Club at Georgia Tech is raising money for a new fleet of Club 420s to be purchased this year.  We are excited about this upcoming purchase and value your continued support. 

Friends of Georgia Tech Sailing,

We are excited to announce that we are going to purchase a new fleet of 6 C420s during this school year. This is a very exciting time for our club and we hope that these boats will provide further opportunities for us to host even more intercollegiate regattas and events for the Georgia Tech community.  As of now, we are about halfway to our fundraising goal of $20,000.  We appreciate your continued support for Georgia Tech Sailing and we hope to see you at one of our upcoming events this school year.

If you have any questions regarding donations or would like to learn more about boat naming and/or sponsorship opportunities, please feel free to contact the executive board.  We hope that you will consider making a tax deductible donation.

Thanks,

The Sailing Club at Georgia Tech Executive Board


UPDATE:

We are currently in the final push to have this money raised by the end of 2017. We just need $1,500 in order to meet our goal. 

With this final push, we are anticipating being able to keep our current fleet in addition to buying the new fleet. In the past two years, we've seen club membership jump from 40 members per semester on average to nearly 60 members this semester. Having 12 boats will help us to get more people out on the water and allow us to teach more new members how to sail.

If you're looking to make a larger donation check out the "How to Donate" section below. There are instructions for making tax-deductible donations. If you're interested in purchasing any Georgia Tech Sailing Gear, head over to the Merchandise page under the Support tab.  If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to the Georgia Tech Sailing Executive Board (gtsailingexec@googlegroups.com).

How to Donate

Donations are tax-deductible through the Georgia Tech Foundation.  You can either donate online or mail a check.

DONATE ONLINE

Donate Online

1) Find the "Donate Now" button at the top of the Georgia Tech Foundation webpage.
2) Enter your gift amount at the top and again in the "Other Designation" box.
3) In the "If other, please specify" box, put Sailing Club at Georgia Tech.

Mail a Check

Make the check out to the Georgia Tech Foundation and write Georgia Tech Sailing in the memo line
